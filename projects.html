League of Legends Projects

These projects were mainly done to learn Python, especially the requests library and APIs with a platform I was familiar with.

League Discord Bot
A discord bot which gives information about summoners, their masteries, their ingame opponents, and much more.

Game Outcome Predictor
A collaborative project with dungwoong where we try to predict the outcome of a given League of Legends Game. Accuracy ~70%.

Stock Analysis

Stock analysis was mainly done to apply my knowledge of numpy, apndas, and matplotlib. Correlations between various stocks and other economic factors were compared and graphed.
