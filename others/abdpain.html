Unknown Illness - (June 2021 - ongoing)

Here is a summary of what happened in this Darwinian-Kafkaesque nightmare. Basic info on the right.

June 16

On Amoxicillin. From what I remember the dosage was rather strong. Treating tooth infection.

June 23

Finished Prescribed Amoxicillin. Was supposed to get root canal this day but a sore appeared on back of throat, so to be cautious we decide to wait for sore to leave before procedure.

June 25

At around 7pm a stomach ache started. All around abdomen. Felt more painful than the average stomach ache so turned in early at around 9 or 10.

June 26

Stomachache still there, which almost never happens. Noticed sneezing and peeing/pooping made pain worse. Stool was rather odd. It would usually start out as pellets then come out as two or three logs that looked kinda cracked.

June 30

Decided to see a doctor (we will call them D1). D1 diagnosed me wtih constipation, and I was told to drink lots of water, eat vegetables and try IBGard if thigns don't improve.

Around this time I also notice that the pains eems to be primarily on lower left and right abdomen, with slight pain upper center. (Later doctor told me this is colon and stomach)

July 3

Diet change had no affect, so took IBGard before sleeping. Woke up to unbearable pain that kept me up from around 2:30 - 4:00AM.

July 4

Saw walk-in doctor (D2). Gave me forms for a few blood tests, urine test, xray, ultrasound. H. pylori stool test was given later but could not be taken due to recent antibiotics

Completed Blood and Urine test.

July 5

Did xray (chest and abdomen).

July 6

Did ultrasound (full abdomen).

July 8

Saw doctor (D3) from same clinic as D2. All tests came back normal. No celiac disease or infection found. Stool was found in colon

Prescribed Senna Tablets, Restoralax, and Metamucil.

Took all three and noticed pressure inside butt. Stool did come out much softer.

July 9

After taking Restoralax got unbearable pain in abdomen. Stopped taking Restoralax.

July 10

Saw doctor (D4) at different clinic who told me to stop taking Senna Tablets and Restoralax. Prescribed antacid Sandoz Pantaprazole 20mg once daily.

July 11

Took antacids. Seemed to help a bit.

July 12

After dinner I had considerable bloating and abdomen swelled up a lot. A lot of internal pressure.

Had bowel movement with dry stool. Having to pee every hour.

July 13

Saw doctor (D5) at same clinic as D2,D3.
